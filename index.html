Youth Risk Assessment Tool

Assess risk quickly and clearly

No data is saved or stored. Scoring runs locally in your browser; nothing is transmitted.
Takes approximately 5–10 minutes to complete.
Answer Key — How to Interpret Each Option
  • No Info
    Information not available to make a judgment.
  • No
    Information available and definitively not present.
  • Maybe
    Information available and might be present.
  • Yes
    Information available and definitively present.

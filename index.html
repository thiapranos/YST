York-Simcoe Sex Trafficking Screener (YST)

Screen for sex trafficking risk quickly and clearly

Responses are not saved on this website. Local saving options are available, subject to your agency procedure.
Takes approximately 10–15 minutes to complete.

What results you’ll get

  • ST Exploitation Warning Signs score – indicates that a young person may be experiencing trafficking-related exploitation or harms now.
  • ST Recruitment Warning Signs score – indicates that a young person may be targeted, groomed, or at increased risk of recruitment.

These scores support—rather than replace—your clinical judgement and safety planning. They are not a diagnosis.

Prior to starting screener, be aware of these known groups at increased risk More info
  • Youth is from a racialized background.
  • Youth is from an indigenous background.
  • Youth has a developmental disability.
  • Youth identifies as LGBTQ+.

This context should be considered alongside—but not replace—the item-by-item judgments in the screener.
